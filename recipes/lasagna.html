Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Sheets
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Cooking Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
  6. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
